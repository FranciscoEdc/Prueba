TailsOS

Translations:[ES],[PT-EU]

It is a Debian-based Linux distribution that is installed on an external drive & offers anonimity via Tor, with a decent app kit, memory in the own external drive where you can save files you want to preserve &, once you shutdown the device, everything you did on it will be cleaned.

It is specially benefficial for domestic-abuse victims & activists under oppressive regimes so that the malefactors cannot control them as easily. Furthermore, TailsOS does not require many resources, thus you can use it in many PCs.
